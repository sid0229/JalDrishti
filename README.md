# JalDrishti 💧

**Real-Time Groundwater Monitoring & Decision Support Platform**

JalDrishti is a real-time groundwater monitoring platform that leverages data from **5,260 Digital Water Level Recorder (DWLR) stations** across India to provide actionable insights on groundwater availability, recharge patterns, and water-level trends. The platform empowers policymakers, researchers, field officers, and local communities with accessible, data-driven tools for sustainable groundwater management.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 📊 Real-Time Groundwater Monitoring

* Continuous monitoring of groundwater levels using DWLR station data.
* Real-time visualization of water-level fluctuations and trends.
* Dynamic estimation of groundwater recharge.

### 🗺️ Interactive Visualizations

* Easy-to-understand graphs and trend analysis.
* Geographic maps displaying groundwater conditions across regions.
* Historical and real-time data comparison.

### 🚨 Early Warning Alerts

* Push notifications for:

  * Sudden groundwater drawdowns
  * Drought risk indicators
  * Significant recharge events
* Timely alerts to support proactive decision-making.

### 🌐 Regional Language Support

* Multi-language interface for wider accessibility.
* Groundwater insights and notifications available in regional languages.

### ♿ Accessibility for All

* Voice assistance support.
* Screen-reader compatibility.
* Inclusive design for users with disabilities.

### 🤖 AI Chatbot Assistant

* Answers groundwater-related queries.
* Explains trends and insights in simple language.
* Supports multiple regional languages.

### 🤝 NGO Partnerships

* Enables community-level awareness and intervention.
* Facilitates on-ground support when critical groundwater issues are detected.

### 📱 Mobile-First Experience

* Designed for ease of use in urban and rural areas.
* Optimized for field officers, farmers, researchers, and policymakers.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Groundwater is a critical resource, yet monitoring and understanding its status remains challenging due to fragmented data, limited accessibility, and delayed decision-making.

JalDrishti addresses these challenges by:

* Centralizing groundwater data from DWLR stations.
* Providing real-time monitoring and visualization.
* Making insights accessible through regional languages and inclusive design.
* Delivering early warnings to support proactive action.

---

## 🏗️ Proposed Solution

JalDrishti collects and analyzes high-frequency groundwater level data from 5,260 DWLR stations. The platform transforms raw data into meaningful insights through:

* Real-time groundwater tracking
* Recharge estimation
* Trend visualization
* Early warning systems
* AI-powered assistance
* Community support through NGO collaboration
